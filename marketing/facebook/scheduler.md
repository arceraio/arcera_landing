## Posting Best Practices

### Optimal Times (Based on LA audience)
- **Best:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9am-11am or 1pm-3pm
- **Good:** Monday-Friday, 6pm-8pm
- **Avoid:** Weekends (unless community-focused)

### Engagement Tips
- Ask questions in comments
- Respond within 1 hour
- Use 1-2 relevant hashtags: #LAHomeowners #HomeInsurance #Arcera
- Tag location when relevant

### A/B Testing
- Test headlines (emotional vs. practical)
- Test CTAs ("Start Free" vs. "Learn More" vs. "Get Protected")
- Test images (home photos vs. app screenshots vs. graphics)
