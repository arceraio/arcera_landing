Privacy Policy

How we handle your data—and why.

1. Introduction

Arcera Digital ("Arcera," "we," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our digital property documentation platform.

By using Arcera, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Property details (address, property type, description)
  • Documentation you upload (photos, videos, receipts, serial numbers)
  • Communications with our support team

Automatically collected information:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system, device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your transactions and manage your subscription
  • Store and organize your property documentation
  • Power AI features (categorization, search, analysis)
  • Communicate with you about service updates and support
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Ownership

You own your data. All property documentation, photos, videos, and information you upload to Arcera remains your property.

We access your data only to:

  • Provide the services you've requested
  • Improve our AI features (with your consent)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal requirements

5. CCPA Privacy Rights (California)

California Consumer Privacy Act

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: You may request what personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@arcera.digital

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or government request
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your property documentation with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Arcera uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Required for account authentication and service functionality
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how you use our service so we can improve it
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect service functionality.

8. AI & Automated Processing

Arcera uses AI to help categorize and analyze your property documentation. AI processing happens on your data only to provide service features (search, organization, suggestions).

We will not use your property documentation to train or improve AI models unless you explicitly opt in. Your data remains yours.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with access controls
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring
  • Employee access controls and training

No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

After account deletion, we may retain certain information in anonymized form for analytics and improvement of our services—but this data cannot identify you.

11. Children's Privacy

Arcera is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

12. Your Choices

You can:

  • Access and update account information through your settings
  • Download your data using our export feature
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the service. Your continued use after such notice constitutes acceptance.
