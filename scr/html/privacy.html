8. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, the CCPA as amended by the CPRA provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. We summarize your six major rights using the acronym L-O-C-K-E-D:

RightDescription
L — Right to LIMIT You have the right to direct us to limit our use and disclosure of your sensitive personal information to only what is necessary to provide you with our services.
O — Right to OPT-OUT You have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. (We do not sell or share your data.)
C — Right to CORRECT You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information we have about you.
K — Right to KNOW You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
E — Right to EQUAL TREATMENT You have the right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights. We will not discriminate against you.
D — Right to DELETE You have the right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
